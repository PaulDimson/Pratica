Un ottimo e indimenticabile Thanksgiving

Deliziosa focaccia farcita alla cipolla, formaggio brie e mirtilli

Un impatto dirompente di sapori salati e dolci perfettamente bilanciati con la cipolla caramellata quindi con un retrogusto dolce dato da un alimento salato accompagnato dai dolci mirtilli resa cremosa dal brie. Una perfetta ricetta per un perfetto ringraziamento!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flow
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scape rind off with paring knife if desire, though it's not necessary)
  • 11 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour.....
